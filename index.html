About

KaiTea is a novice tea importer based in Richmond, VA that custom blends various organic teas. Our teas are carefully crafted from naturally grown herbs, each one offering a unique story.

The seeds that formed the foundation of KaiTea were first planted in 2018 when Ashanté (or Kai) began her journey of studying Ayurvedic practices such as yoga and meditation. She found that consuming tea often aids with achieving mindfulness and is a universally loved beverage. Since she also became a barista around the same time, she often deals with crafting beverages with unique ingredients. Each year since, Ashanté aims to create more unique concoctions to express her love of Ayurveda and beverage crafting.

Please browse through our products and look at life through our lens for just a moment.
